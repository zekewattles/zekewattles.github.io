→ This webpage is typeset in Platz. Platz began as an exploration of monospace characteristics within a text typeface. The name, Platz, is an ambiguous word in German meaning place, space, location. It can often be seen on the names of streets, for example. If typography can convey an sense of place, as it is often said, Platz is an impressionistic Berlin from the perspective of a Californian. It’s currently at one weight with over 440 unique glyphs. A full release is slated for late 2019.
