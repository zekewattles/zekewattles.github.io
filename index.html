*This webpage is typeset in a beta version of Platz. Platz began as an exploration of monospace characteristics within a text font & evolved to become an all-purpose text face inspired by the typographic landscape of Berlin. Its technical, engineered qualities are balanced by humanistic proportions, strong contrast, & comfortable spacing for extended reading text. It has closed terminals, flat stoke endings, & short descenders for tight typesetting if desired. Platz is currently one weight with over 450 unique glyphs, full accents, & case-sensitive forms. Full release (with additional weights & italics) planned for late 2019.
