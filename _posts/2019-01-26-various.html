Category: Print, Identity, Packaging, Art Direction

Mike Sierra Tango

A series of type specimen posters for the HMCT’s “MST” exhibition on multi-script typefaces. Shown at ATypI Tokyo 2019.

I dissected the type specimen booklets for Plex and Kazimer provided by the designers and created a hierarchical system for each for the space of the poster.

Upside Hats

Branding, packaging, and design direction for a Portland-based startup with a uniquely customizable product.

A hat with velcro monospace letters can be rearranged to create a new message every day.
