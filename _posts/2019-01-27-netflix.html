Please Binge Responsibly

Chill by Netflix is a fresh, bold, and accessible line of sexual personal care products from Netflix. It’s an opportunity for Netflix to finally own “Netflix and Chill” while promoting safe sex to their audience, which is predominantly Gen Z and Millennials. The line includes tingling body lotion, personal lubricant, unisex fragrance, a massage candle, and natural latex condoms. Products are sold online as a kit, and customers receive them in a unique cardboard mailer box featuring cheeky illustrated quotes from Netflix Originals that relate to sex and dating. Please binge responsibly.
