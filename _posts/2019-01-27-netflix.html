A brand extension for Netflix in the personal care and sexual wellness market.

Category: Packaging
Instructors: Andrew Gibbs, Jessica Deseo
Year: 2017

Binging is Better Together

Netflix’s brand is known for its award-winning content as much as it is its unofficial catchphrase: Netflix and chill? This project proposes a way for Netflix to take ownership of ”chill” through playful humor. The packages features quotes from Netflix original shows that relate to sex and dating.

Sketching process

The form language of the packaging is derived from the shape of the Netflix logo. The curve allows for comfortable grip and easy pouring.
