A Sunset You Can Touch

So how do you communicate the power of the sun? By letting light speak for itself. The blade is a visceral experience of pure light—a massive slice of sunset, on loop.

The back of the blade functions as seating. Relax and watch the sunset — then, the second sunset begins. It’s like watching a sunset that you can walk right up to and touch. The power of this lighting is its relation to nature.

LA → Berlin → Worldwide

Five top graphic design students and one instructor travel to Berlin to concept and design an interactive installation for a tech client. The client had something really unique: a new type of LED with the power of the sun.

What do I mean by that? Most commercial lighting operates at a relatively limited spectrum — it’s why you might get a headache after sitting under fluorescent lights for too long. All light has a color temperature — it’s also why golden hour looks so good. Somehow, this company managed to design a type of LED that could replicate the deepest reds and the coolest blues, at nearly the full range of natural light. And at low energy input. That’s a pretty big deal.

Process

Models, SketchUp, projection mapping, DesertX (scale), testing & even coding for LEDs, pitching in Berlin!
